zenly your world

Zenly (Snap. Inc) is a live social map of your friends and family. To date, it has 40 million+ MAUs globally. Top #10 download worldwide.

My 1.8 years at Zenly as a Product Designer is an incredible journey of learning and creating with the most talented design team.

Beyond shipping features that are used by millions of users, I also craft delights for user love.
