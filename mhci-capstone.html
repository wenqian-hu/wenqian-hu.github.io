Initial Problem Statement

Why is there a drop-off at Discover Student Loans' product selection page?

Stakeholder Map

Primary Research

We conducted a user-centered research process to explore the given problem and contextualize it within a framework of deeper customer needs. We conducted several rounds of exploratory & generative research with key stakeholder groups, including 14 students, 13 parents and 12 educators in total.

Guerrilla Research

We printed screenshots of 4 repayment options from the DSL website and asked students which one they would choose in an informal setting.

Interview

We used semi-structured interview techniques to learn how various stakeholders perceive finance and loan-related topics.

Card Sorting

We prepared a list of finance-related nouns on cards and asked high school students to categorize those cards in whatever way made the most sense to them.

Intercept

To know more about the parent-student dynamic, we intercepted 5 pairs of high school students and their parents, who were just finishing up a CMU campus tour.¬†
