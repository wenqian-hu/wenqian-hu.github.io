// BRKN

Tisch School of the Arts, New York, 2018

Course: Analogue Photography, Black and White

Instructor: Terry E Boddie

I wanted to express the contradiction and struggles of the protagonist lying under the soft, peaceful texture through creating an illusory scene in a dreamy atmosphere. The illogical sequences narrates the fragments of her dream, piecing together what she is experiencing in reality - connection with ancestor, secret message from anonymous. I intended to use mirror in this series, for it is a form of a reflection, a medium of illusion and dreams, and a portal to escape from the present. The interaction between my protagonist and the environment tempts to explore and express the emotions that mirrors and reflections might convey as significant symbols. It is surreal, broken, and irrational.
