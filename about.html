Jackie Hu

A Cosmopolitan Designer

- - - - -

I first study design and art in a cosmopolitan school, where I get to travel and work with people in different countries. My experience teaches me:

Good design is a universal lanaguage and a global passport.

I spend my time at NYU studying Interactive Media Arts, experimenting with all kinds of emerging technologies. While the digital world is full of treasures, I want to make the experience more accessible, comprehensive, and seamless to people who are using them. That’s how I earned my Master’s degree in Human-Computer Interaction at CMU later on.

As technology constantly shapes, modifies, and revolutionizes our cultures, I believe that designers have the mission to make meaningful design decisions and always put people at the heart ❤️ of our work.
