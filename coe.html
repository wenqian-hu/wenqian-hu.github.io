Center of the Earth

Interactive easter egg made for Halloween

about

Duration

3 months

My Role

Ideation

Art Direction

What are inside the center of the earth? What are the fun interactions possible when playing with a map?

I collaborated with 3D artist and iOS developer to create a Halloween-themed scene when users zoom in to the end of the map -- an interactive pumpkin chorus.

By tapping on each pumpkin, a music note will be triggered. If you play the pumpkins in certain order, they will sing the Star War song all together :)
