Project Description

The Smile Wall is an interactive Internet Art project that collects smiling short videos of people come from different culture backgrounds. Participants are from 9 countries, and their smiles all convey a friendly message towards audience. Smile is a world common language that can be understood by everyone.

Make A Compliment

By typing in a postive adjective, a person in the frame will smile because of your nice word ;)
