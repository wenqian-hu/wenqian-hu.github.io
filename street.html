"You don't make a photograph just with a camera. You bring to the act of photography all the pictures you have seen, the books you have read, the music you have heard, the people you have loved"
— Ansel Adams

I love traveling with my analog film cameras. I am constantly intrigued by the unique connections drawn between people and cities. Similar to design, street photography requires eyes to observe, ears to listen, and a heart to create resonation. I use photography as a way to understand and capture how people interact with others and their surrounding environments.
